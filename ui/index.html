About
Poetry is created from the soul. The language of poetry is the language of emotions. It has the power to motivate people. People often say that motivation doesn't last long. Well, neither does bathing - that's why we recommend it daily. PoetryMela is the place where you can find lots of poems by various indian poets which help you to refresh your mind. Not only this, PoetryMela is also there to ignite your passion. You can share your own poems too. Let your imagination run wild, after all, it is your canvas.
